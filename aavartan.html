Aavartan

AAVARTAN’14, presided by DR. N.M NAGARKAR, DIRECTOR, AIIMS RAIPUR, was a grand success witnessed by over 30,000 participants from all over the country. The students from IIT ROPAR, IIT KGP, NIT WARANGAL etc. participated in offline and online events of ROBOTICS, SCIENCE EXHIBTIONS, AERODYNAMICS, QUIZ etc. AAVARTAN ’14 was not just a fest, it was a revolution in engineering to excavate the best out of the Indian brains. SCIENCE EXHIBITIONS were the evidence of the intellectuality of the human mind.

It was a revolution which aimed to promote technical knowledge, attract and develop the talented indigenous human resource and incorporate the spirit of scientific enquiry among the students. For an engineer it’s not just sufficient to solve the problem; he also has to ensure that the implementation of his solution is practical and economical. Aavartan’14 not only added values to the life but also gave a platform to the students to dive into the depth of technical knowledge and to fathom the jewels of knowledge. It gave technically inclined youth, a national level stage where their talent and expertise would be recognized and duly rewarded. It was a 3 day journey where participants not only learnt, but explored the undiscovered path of science and technology and reached the new heights of business ideas.
