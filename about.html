About Us

The Technocracy is the technical committee of NIT Raipur. It organizes various events whole round the academic year for technical development of student and provides student with platform to build, improve and test the implementation of engineering knowledge and skills. Technocracy is also responsible for organization of AAVARTAN, tech fest of college whose soul lies in “Vigyaan”, the student exhibition. It provides an opportunity to students to face real industrial challenges by giving them problem statement from industries. The Technocracy’ is managed by a group of faculty members and student Coordinator, who takes the major decisions regarding the planning and execution of the events. Every year, committee works towards the realization of a pre-defined vision, which drives and guides the organizers for the entire year. Technical committee brand name carries with it the responsibility of providing the best and the latest in science and technology to the nation
