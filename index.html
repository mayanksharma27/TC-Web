The Technical Committee of NIT Raipur

Welcome To Technocracy

Team TechnoCracy” is a group of zealous students of NIT Raipur, who use their scientific skill to take Indian engineering to new heights. Implementing ideas and shaping them into live structures is what engineers do. THE TECHNOCRACY has always believed in giving students a platform to bring out their ideas and turn them into reality. The historical success of Aavartan proves this fact. Be it the internships for bright students or opportunities to succeed in various others off-campus events. Aavartan has provided students with vast options to choose among. The team along with GO GREEN also participates in ECLECTIKA - NIT Raipur’s cultural-tech festival. TEAM AAVARTAN deals with the organization of various technically challenging events and guest lectures by eminent dignitaries to dig out the talent hidden among young innovative minds and to give a platform to students to showcase their knowledge.

"The team is assisted by the faculty as well. AAVARTAN is all about bringing about a “Revolution in Technology”, it is a democracy of technical knowledge, that is, technology of the pupil, for the pupil and by the pupil."
